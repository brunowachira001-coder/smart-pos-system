📋 CALCULATIONS

Net Revenue = Σ (price × quantity) for all sales
Net Cost = units_sold × cost_price
Net Profit = Net Revenue - Net Cost
Profit Margin = (Net Profit / Net Revenue) × 100
Return Rate = (returned_units / units_sold) × 100
