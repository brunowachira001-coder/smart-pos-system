📱 Install Smart Traders Inventory

Follow these steps to install the app on your device

🖥️ Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

  • Visit the website in Chrome or Edge browser
  • Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar
  • Click the icon and select "Install"
  • The app will be added to your desktop and start menu

📱 Android (Chrome)

  • Open the website in Chrome browser
  • Tap the menu (⋮) in the top right
  • Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  • Tap "Install" in the popup
  • The app icon will appear on your home screen

🍎 iPhone/iPad (Safari)

  • Open the website in Safari browser
  • Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom
  • Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • Tap "Add" in the top right
  • The app icon will appear on your home screen
