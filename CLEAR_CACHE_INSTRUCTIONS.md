## 🔧 MANUAL METHODS (If Above Doesn't Work)

### For Chrome on Android:
1. Open Chrome
2. Tap the 3 dots (⋮) in top right
3. Tap "Settings"
4. Tap "Privacy and security"
5. Tap "Clear browsing data"
6. Select "Cached images and files"
7. Tap "Clear data"
8. Go back to the site

### For Safari on iPhone:
1. Open Settings app (not Safari browser)
2. Scroll down and tap "Safari"
3. Scroll down and tap "Clear History and Website Data"
4. Tap "Clear History and Data" to confirm
5. Open Safari and go to the site

### For Firefox on Mobile:
1. Tap the 3 dots (⋮) menu
2. Tap "Settings"
3. Tap "Delete browsing data"
4. Check "Cached images and files"
5. Tap "Delete browsing data"
6. Go back to the site
