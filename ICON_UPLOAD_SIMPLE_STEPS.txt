═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  📱 UPLOAD YOUR PWA ICONS - SUPER SIMPLE GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

YOU HAVE: 4 icon files on your computer ✅
YOU NEED: Upload them to GitHub → Vercel auto-deploys → Logo appears!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 1: GO TO GITHUB
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open your browser
2. Go to: github.com
3. Sign in to your account
4. Find your project repository (the one with this code)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 2: NAVIGATE TO PUBLIC FOLDER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click on "public" folder in the file list
2. You'll see files like: manifest.json, sw.js, etc.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 3: UPLOAD YOUR 4 ICONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click "Add file" button (top right corner)
2. Click "Upload files"
3. Drag and drop your 4 icon files:
   
   📁 pwa-icon-192.png
   📁 pwa-icon-512.png
   📁 pwa-icon-maskable-192.png
   📁 pwa-icon-maskable-512.png

4. Type commit message: "Add PWA icons"
5. Click "Commit changes" (green button)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 4: WAIT FOR VERCEL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⏱️  Wait 1-2 minutes for Vercel to automatically deploy
✅ You'll see a notification when it's done

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 5: TEST ON YOUR PHONE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open your website on mobile
2. Clear browser cache (Settings → Privacy → Clear data)
3. Delete the old app from home screen (long-press → remove)
4. Go back to website → Click "Install App"
5. Check home screen → LOGO SHOULD APPEAR! 🎉

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  QUICK CHECK: Are Icons Uploaded?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Visit these URLs in your browser:
→ your-site.vercel.app/pwa-icon-192.png
→ your-site.vercel.app/pwa-icon-512.png

If you see the icons, they're uploaded! ✅
