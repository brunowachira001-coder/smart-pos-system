# Smart POS System - Phase 6: Analytics & Reporting

## Overview
Phase 6 focuses on comprehensive analytics and reporting that transforms operational data into actionable business intelligence. This phase delivers decision-making insights through detailed reports, interactive dashboards, and flexible export options.

## Phase 6 Objectives

### 1. Sales Reports

#### 1.1 Daily Sales Report
- **Total Sales**: Sum of all transactions
- **Sales by Payment Method**: Cash, M-Pesa, Bank, Credit breakdown
- **Sales by Category**: Revenue per category
- **Sales by Product**: Top 10 products by revenue
- **Sales by Staff**: Sales per cashier
- **Sales by Branch**: Sales per location
- **Transactions Count**: Number of transactions
- **Average Transaction Value**: Total sales / transaction count
- **Comparison**: vs yesterday, vs last week, vs last month, vs last year

#### 1.2 Weekly Sales Report
- **Total Sales**: Sum of all transactions for the week
- **Daily Breakdown**: Sales for each day of the week
- **Sales Trends**: Trending up/down vs previous week
- **Best Day**: Highest sales day of the week
- **Worst Day**: Lowest sales day of the week
- **Category Performance**: Top/bottom categories
- **Product Performance**: Top/bottom products
- **Staff Performance**: Top/bottom staff
- **Comparison**: vs previous week, vs same week last month, vs same week last year

#### 1.3 Monthly Sales Report
- **Total Sales**: Sum of all transactions for the month
- **Weekly Breakdown**: Sales for each week
- **Daily Average**: Average daily sales
- **Sales Trends**: Trending up/down vs previous month
- **Best Week**: Highest sales week
- **Worst Week**: Lowest sales week
- **Category Performance**: Top/bottom categories
- **Product Performance**: Top/bottom products
- **Staff Performance**: Top/bottom staff
- **Comparison**: vs previous month, vs same month last year

#### 1.4 Custom Period Reports
- **Date Range Selection**: Select any date range
- **Comparison Periods**: Compare to previous period
- **Granularity**: Daily, weekly, monthly breakdown
- **Filters**: By category, product, staff, branch, payment method
- **Metrics**: Revenue, transactions, average value, growth rate

### 2. Profit Tracking (True Profit, Not Just Revenue)

#### 2.1 Profit Calculation
- **Revenue**: Total sales amount
- **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**: Sum of product costs
- **Gross Profit**: Revenue - COGS
- **Gross Profit Margin**: (Gross Profit / Revenue) * 100
- **Operating Expenses**: Staff, rent, utilities, etc.
- **Net Profit**: Gross Profit - Operating Expenses
- **Net Profit Margin**: (Net Profit / Revenue) * 100

#### 2.2 Profit by Category
- **Category Revenue**: Total sales per category
- **Category COGS**: Total cost per category
- **Category Gross Profit**: Revenue - COGS per category
- **Category Gross Margin**: Margin per category
- **Category Net Profit**: After operating expenses
- **Category Net Margin**: Net margin per category
- **Profitability Ranking**: Categories ranked by profit

#### 2.3 Profit by Product
- **Product Revenue**: Total sales per product
- **Product COGS**: Total cost per product
- **Product Gross Profit**: Revenue - COGS per product
- **Product Gross Margin**: Margin per product
- **Product Net Profit**: After allocated expenses
- **Product Net Margin**: Net margin per product
- **Profitability Ranking**: Products ranked by profit

#### 2.4 Profit Trends
- **Daily Profit**: Profit for each day
- **Weekly Profit**: Profit for each week
- **Monthly Profit**: Profit for each month
- **Profit Trend**: Trending up/down
- **Profit Forecast**: Projected profit
- **Profit Variance**: Actual vs budget

### 3. Product Performance Analytics

#### 3.1 Product Sales Analysis

- **Price Variance**: Actual vs standard price
- **Discount Impact**: Total discounts applied
- **Net Revenue**: Revenue after discounts

#### 3.2 Product Profitability
- **Cost per Unit**: Product cost
- **Gross Profit per Unit**: Selling price - cost
- **Gross Margin per Unit**: Margin percentage
- **Total Gross Profit**: Units sold * margin
- **Profitability Ranking**: Products ranked by profit
- **High Margin Products**: Products with high margins
- **Low Margin Products**: Products with low margins

#### 3.3 Product Performance Metrics
- **Sell-through Rate**: Units sold / units available
- **Inventory Turnover**: How fast product sells
- **Days to Sell**: Average days to sell inventory
- **Stock-out Incidents**: Times product was out of stock
- **Return Rate**: Returns as % of sales
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Based on returns/complaints

#### 3.4 Product Recommendations
- **Best Sellers**: Top 10 products by sales
- **Profit Leaders**: Top 10 products by profit
- **Slow Movers**: Products with low sales
- **Dead Stock**: Products with no sales
- **Reorder Candidates**: Products below reorder point
- **Discontinue Candidates**: Products to remove
