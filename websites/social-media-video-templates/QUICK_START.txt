HOW TO USE
================================================================================

1. OPEN THE APP
   - Open index.html in your web browser
   - You'll see 6 template cards

2. CHOOSE A TEMPLATE
   - Click on any template
   - A modal will open with customization options

3. FILL IN YOUR CONTENT
   - Product name, price, description
   - Customer testimonials
   - Sale details
   - Tips and tricks
   - Announcements

4. CHOOSE A COLOR
   - Select from 5 color schemes
   - See preview update in real-time

5. PREVIEW
   - See your video template in the preview
   - Make adjustments as needed

6. GENERATE & DOWNLOAD
   - Click "Generate Video"
   - Template downloads as image

7. CREATE VIDEO
   - Open CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or Canva
   - Import the downloaded template
   - Add music, effects, transitions
   - Export as video

8. POST ON SOCIAL MEDIA
   - Upload to Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
   - Add captions and hashtags
   - Monitor engagement
