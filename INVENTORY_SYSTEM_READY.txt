   → Fill in all fields (name, SKU, prices, stock)
   → Click "Add Product"
   → Product should appear in the table

3. Edit a Product:
   → Click "•••" menu on any product
   → Click "Edit"
   → Update any fields
   → Click "Update Product"

4. Restock a Product:
   → Click "•••" menu on any product
   → Click "Restock"
   → Enter quantity to add
   → Click "Restock"

5. Adjust Stock:
   → Click "•••" menu on any product
   → Click "Adjust Stock"
   → Enter positive (add) or negative (subtract) number
   → Add reason (optional)
   → Click "Adjust Stock"

6. Check Analytics:
   → Navigate to: /inventory-analytics
   → Verify accurate cost/profit calculations
   → Check low stock alerts

7. Export Data:
   → Click "Export" button on inventory page
   → CSV file should download
